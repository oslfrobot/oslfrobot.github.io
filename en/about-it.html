About it

This robot was ask at LSTME, (Letné sústredenie talentovanej mládeže v elektronike) it is summer camp for students who are interested in robotics, programming, 3D modeling and electronics. We have to make robot that can follow line, at first then we have to make it, that he can be calibrated autonomiously and then object avoiding. We made it all and it is working very good, you need only to go to our documentation and then you might be able to make your own at home for funny sum of cash. We hope, you can use it to learn.
